Your wedding is a momentous event for your family and loved ones. It is so important you take the time to celebrate every single moment with them.

Bespoke Weddings will reflect the personality and style of our client. Our philosophy is deeply focused on exceptional client service, family values and embracing desi traditions. You and your loved ones deserve an unforgettable event with the best memories.

Whether you’re looking to have a simple intimate ceremony or a big fat Desi wedding, all weddings can benefit from a professional planner or coordinator. The weeks prior to your wedding and the wedding day itself will fly by as is. Save yourself hundreds of hours in research and preparation, and help us bring your vision to life. If any issues or questions arise, our wedding coordinator will be the go-to person to save the day.

We manage your big day from start to finish in an organized and timely manner, serving as the point person for all the vendors, representing your wants and needs while you get to enjoy your day to the max.

Hira Niaz

CEO and Director
Prior to starting her own company, Hira Niaz was working in the corporate industry where the majority of her work revolved around planning events. From trunk shows and fundraisers to conferences and Gala dinners, she has a growing passion for the events industry. The idea for Bespoke Weddings started after she realized the importance of having a wedding coordinator for her own celebrations. With a positive attitude, and attention to detail, she will make sure that her clients once in a lifetime planning experience is fun and exciting but absolutely stress free. She believes that couples should not miss out on any beautiful moments on their special day.

When she is not planning events, Hira is working on her makeup and beauty blog. She loves to cook for her family, travel and explore new places.

Contact Hira today to schedule your free in person consultation.

Zaid Haque

Creative Lead
As a professional designer and serial entrepreneur, Zaid helps out with all the creative details that complement the actual Big Day. Working on many startups as well as tech companies, he’s held positions in both design and business roles. Custom fit Services

Bespoke Weddings is all about creating memorable experiences, truly unique, and one-of-a-kind for each couple. With our custom-made packages, we are committed to taking care of all your organizational needs, without you or your family and friends having to worry about all the details. Your wedding should be stress-free where everything you imagined comes together flawlessly.

At Bespoke Weddings, we offer a range of wedding planning services; from budget planning, venue selection, invitations and photography, vendor research, guest management, and day of coordination. Our services differ depending on how far along you are in the process of planning your wedding. Both our packages include unlimited consultation and support via phone and email and a detailed timeline leading up to the event.

Each wedding is unique which is why we can tailor our services to create a bespoke package for you that best suits your needs. Our main focus is to make sure your day flows according to your expectations!

Partial Planning and Coordination

For this package, the planning work is generally shared 50/50 between the wedding couple and the Bespoke Weddings Team.

This package is for the couple who desires to plan their own wedding day but would like ongoing access to vendor referrals and detailed assistance with all elements related to design and decor along with Month-of Coordination.

This package is optimal for clients who want to be involved in the process, but need help bringing all of the details together into a seamless event. We provide guidance and expertise along the way so you can feel confidence about the planning process. We’ll keep you on track to get everything completed on time.

Month/Day-of Coordination

Our Month-Of Coordination package is ideal for couples who have done most of the planning and vendor hiring but need a professional and guidance when the stress starts to set in the month prior to the wedding, and, more importantly, on the wedding day.

Four to eight weeks prior to your wedding, we confirm the final details with you and all vendors involved (i.e. Venue, Photographer/Videographer, Makeup & Hair Stylists, Florist and etc.) and will manage the entire event making sure everything goes smoothly so the bride & groom can enjoy this important day of their life with a peace of mind.

A La Carte Services

Regardless of your budget, we also offer a la carte services according to your needs such as:

  • Invitation Designs

  • Bridal & Groom Attire Arrangements
